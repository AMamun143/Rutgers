About Me

My name is Abdullah Mamun. I have been in the customer service industry for the last 8 years. My most recent experience is working as an account manager for Red Bull. One reason i particularly enjoy this business, and the challenges that i go along with it, is the opportunity to connect with people. I always wanted to be a Airport Manager since i was a kid and that's why i went to Vaughn college of Aeronautics and technology in Queens, New York. I first saw one of my friends creating a web page for his class project during my 4th year of college and i thought that was cool. Since then i started to get into coding. I also took a class on html and css in college but it was too late for me to change my major as it was my last year in college and have taken all the necessary courses to graduate from college.

My real strength is my attention to detail. I pride myself on my reputation for following through and meeting deadlines. When i commit to doing something, i make sure it gets done, and on time.
